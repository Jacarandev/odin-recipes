Spanish Tortilla

Description

Everybody loves a good spanish tortilla, or simply "tortilla" as we call it in Spain. We will be cooking this delicious and easy to make dish. Enjoy!

Ingredients for 4 people
  • Potatoes 700g
  • Olive oil
  • Onion 300g
  • 6 eggs
  • Salt
Steps
  1. Cut the onion in small pieces and let it cook on low heat for 30 minutes
  2. Wash the potatoes and cut them round in similar sizes
  3. Put the potatoes in a pan with olive oil and let it cook for about 10 minutes
  4. After 10 minutes, get the heat a little bit higher so some of the potatoes get a little bit more cooked
  5. Once the potatoes and the onion are cooked put them together in a bowl
  6. Beat the eggs and add them to the bowl where the potatoes and onion are in
  7. Put 2 or 3 spoonfuls of oil in the pan and add the ingredientes from the bowl
  8. Let the egg fry and turn it around when one side of the tortilla is cooked
  9. If you could successfully turn it around without make it a mess, you made it!
  10. Eat the delish
