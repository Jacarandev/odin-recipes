Gazpacho

Description

Ah, summertime! It's sounds like the perfect time to enjoy some good lunch with friends outside, and nothing feels as good than doing it while drinking fresh gazpacho. Simple and refreshing, let's go at it!

Ingredients for 4 people
  • Tomatoes 1kg
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 pieces of garlic
  • Bread
  • Vinegar
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
Steps
  1. Wash the vegetables and cut them and the garlic
  2. Put them together in a blender vase
  3. Add 6 spoonfuls of oil, 4 spoonfuls of vinegar, a loaf of bread and some water
  4. Blend it all together until it's liquid
  5. Once the you get the desired thickness, put it in the fridge
  6. Serve cold!

Pro tip: If you like thicker gazpacho, add more bread while blending it, and if you like it less thick add more water!
