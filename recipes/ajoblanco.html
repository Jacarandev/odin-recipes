Ajoblanco

Description

This is a somewhat underground spanish dish. The only requirement to eat it is that you have to like garlic. Even better if you LOVE it.

Ingredients for 4 people
  • Bread crumbs 150g
  • Cold water .5l
  • Olive oil 100ml
  • Almonds 100g
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Vinegar
  • Salt
Steps
  1. Soak the bread crumbs in water
  2. Blend the bread crumbs, water, almonds garlic clove, vinegar and salt
  3. While blending, add the olive oil slowly, to get the desired texture
  4. Serve cold!

When making ajoblanco and adding the oil think like if you were making mayonnaise.

Pro-tip: Add some sweet fruit to it before eating it. Grapes, melon...
